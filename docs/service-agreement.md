# Culturally Ai Resume Services Agreement

This agreement outlines the scope, payment terms, revision policy, and client responsibilities for Culturally Ai resume and LinkedIn services.

## 1. Services

Client is purchasing one or more of the following services:

- Resume Refresh: $500
- Executive Resume Build: $750
- Custom LinkedIn Page/Profile: $150

## 2. Scope of Work

### Resume Refresh

For clients who already have a resume and need a stronger, cleaner, more targeted version.

Includes resume rewrite, structure cleanup, ATS-conscious keyword alignment, stronger bullet points, professional formatting, and 3 revision rounds.

### Executive Resume Build

For clients who do not have a resume or need a full resume created from scratch.

Includes detailed resume questionnaire, 30-45 minute strategy call after payment, resume strategy and positioning, full resume writing, ATS-conscious formatting, 3 revision rounds, and 3 example jobs to apply for after resume completion.

### Custom LinkedIn Page/Profile

For clients who need stronger LinkedIn profile copy.

Includes LinkedIn headline, About section, experience positioning, skills guidance, profile polish, and one polish round unless added to a resume package.

## 3. Payment Terms

Payment is required before strategy calls, writing, drafting, or revision work begins.

All sales are final. Culturally Ai does not offer refunds.

## 4. Revision Policy

Resume packages include 3 revision rounds.

Revision rounds cover accuracy, wording, formatting, positioning, and missing details provided during the revision window.

Revisions do not include a full change in career direction, target industry, or resume strategy after drafting has begun. Major changes may require a new project scope and additional fee.

## 5. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to provide accurate information, including work history, dates of employment, job titles, education, certifications, achievements, metrics, target roles, and supporting documents when available.

Client is responsible for reviewing drafts carefully and requesting changes within the revision process.

## 6. No Guarantee

Culturally Ai does not guarantee interviews, job offers, salary increases, recruiter responses, or employment outcomes.

Services are designed to improve the quality, clarity, positioning, and professionalism of the client's resume and/or LinkedIn materials.

## 7. Timeline

Typical timelines:

- Resume Refresh: 3-5 business days after intake completion and payment.
- Executive Resume Build: 5-7 business days after the paid strategy call.
- LinkedIn Profile: 2-3 business days after intake completion and payment.

Timelines may be extended if the client delays intake completion, file submission, strategy call scheduling, or revision feedback.

## 8. Approval

By submitting payment, the client confirms they understand and agree to the scope, no-refund policy, revision process, and no-guarantee statement.
