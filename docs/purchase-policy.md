# Culturally Ai Purchase Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

## Payment

Payment is required before any strategy call, resume drafting, LinkedIn writing, or revision work begins.

Clients should complete the private intake first. After Culturally Ai reviews the intake and confirms the best-fit service, payment details are sent.

## No Refunds

All sales are final.

Culturally Ai does not offer refunds because each service includes custom strategy, review, writing, editing, and time reserved for the client. If changes are needed, they are handled through the included revision process.

## Revisions

Resume packages include 3 revision rounds.

Revision rounds may cover:

- Accuracy
- Wording
- Formatting
- Resume structure
- Positioning
- Missing details provided by the client

Revision rounds do not include a complete change in target role, industry, or resume direction after drafting has begun. Major direction changes may require a new strategy review and additional fee.

The $150 LinkedIn Profile service includes one polish round unless purchased as part of a larger resume package.

## Turnaround Time

Typical timelines:

- Resume Refresh: 3-5 business days after intake completion and payment.
- Executive Resume Build: 5-7 business days after the paid strategy call.
- LinkedIn Profile: 2-3 business days after intake completion and payment.

Turnaround may be delayed if the client does not provide required information, files, job targets, or revision feedback.

## Client Responsibilities

The client is responsible for providing accurate work history, dates, job titles, education, certifications, achievements, and supporting details.

Culturally Ai may strengthen wording, structure, and positioning, but will not knowingly create false credentials, false employment, fake metrics, or misleading claims.

## No Job Guarantee

Culturally Ai does not guarantee interviews, job offers, salary outcomes, recruiter responses, or employment results.

Every resume is built with recruiter-informed strategy, ATS-conscious formatting, and role-specific positioning.
